Hi, I'm Dan

I am 26 years old, and I am a part time student at the University of Canterbury studying a Bachelors in Digital Product Design, while working full time at ANZ New Zealand in IT Support.

I've previously studied Web & UX Design at YooBee where my passion started. I've also spent time studying IT and Cookery (without much success due to past issues with mental health), after leaving study for 4 years and focusing on work, I found my calling with UX/UI design, as I want to be able to use it as a platform to elicit change for the better, with better adoption of practices in design for people with disabilities.

In my spare time I like to play games, and do creative things, however due to my lack of time from study and work commitments I do not get to do as much of this as I would like.

I also hope to someday be able to open a Tabletop Cafe (Board Game Cafe), but at this stage, this is just a distant pipe dream.
